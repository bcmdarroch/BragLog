Brag Log

You did it. Own it.

Starting a new job, entering a new field, embarking on any challenging chapter in your life puts you back at square one. Sometimes it's easy to lose sight of how far you've come. Day after day, you might feel like you're not accomplishing anything. Impostor syndrome might rear its ugly head.

Brag Log helps you shake off that mindset. Everyday, you'll receive a text reminder to log your brag of the day. This can be anything, from learning a new skill to checking off that last item on your to-do list to taking time for some much-needed self care. Brag about something you're proud of, no matter how small. Every Sunday evening, you'll receive a text report of everything you've accomplished that week, reminding you just how much you have to brag about.

Text (470) 719-0499 to start logging your brags!
